# AMJ Messaging Architecture

Date: March 9, 2026

## Objective

Align the new website copy with the real AMJ Ingenieria business proposition presented on the live site:

- AMJ is not only a cybersecurity brand.
- AMJ is a consultative technology partner for empresas e instituciones.
- AMJ sells advisory, implementation, software, and ongoing support.
- The primary commercial motion is consultative: evaluate, recommend, implement, support.

The new site currently over-indexes on polished cybersecurity language and under-explains the actual commercial offer. This document defines the messaging system to correct that.

## Core Business Narrative

### What AMJ should say

AMJ helps empresas protect and improve their technology environments through consulting, cybersecurity, implementation, and local support.

### What AMJ should not sound like

- A generic cyber startup
- A pure software reseller
- A vague "innovation" brand
- A design-first agency with abstract promises

## Positioning

### Category

Consultoria e implementacion en ciberseguridad y tecnologia para empresas.

### Audience

- Empresas medianas y grandes
- Operaciones con infraestructura, endpoints, redes, correo, usuarios y procesos criticos
- Decision makers in TI, operaciones, gerencia, continuidad, riesgo, compliance

### Primary pains

- No claridad sobre su exposicion real
- Herramientas aisladas o mal configuradas
- Falta de acompanamiento experto para implementar y operar soluciones
- Riesgo operacional, interrupciones, incidentes, cumplimiento

### AMJ differentiators

- Equipo local en Chile
- Mas de 15 anos de experiencia
- Enfoque consultivo, no solo venta de licencias
- Implementacion y acompanamiento
- Portafolio de soluciones empresariales, no una sola herramienta

## Sitewide Messaging Rules

### Voice

- Professional
- Direct
- Consultative
- Concrete

### Copy rules

- Lead with business outcomes, not design language
- Use specific verbs: evaluar, implementar, proteger, acompanar, operar
- Reduce generic nouns: innovacion, escudo, tranquilidad, transformacion
- Avoid unsupported claims and decorative metrics
- Keep one main CTA language family across the site

## CTA Hierarchy

### Primary CTA

Solicita una asesoria gratuita

### Secondary CTA options

- Agenda una evaluacion
- Habla con un especialista
- Ver soluciones
- Ver planes

### CTA terms to reduce

- Demo Gratis
- Solicitar Demo

Use "demo" only when the user is clearly evaluating a specific product such as Bitdefender GravityZone. Everywhere else, default to advisory language.

## Brand Promise

### Recommended master promise

AMJ Ingenieria ayuda a empresas a evaluar, implementar y operar soluciones de ciberseguridad y tecnologia con acompanamiento experto en Chile.

### Short version

Consultoria, implementacion y soporte para proteger la operacion de tu empresa.

## Information Architecture by Page

### Homepage role

Explain the whole AMJ offer.

The homepage should answer:

- What does AMJ do?
- Who is it for?
- Why trust AMJ?
- What kinds of services and solutions can I buy?
- What happens if I contact AMJ?

### Product page role

Sell a specific solution within the broader AMJ offer.

The product page should answer:

- What is this product?
- What problem does it solve?
- Why buy it through AMJ instead of directly?
- Which plan is right for me?
- What happens after I request contact?

## Current Copy Problems

### 1. The site is too abstract

Examples:

- "Seguridad Digital Garantizada"
- "Proteccion Empresarial Integral"
- "escudo permanente"

These lines sound polished, but they do not explain the actual service model or decision value.

### 2. The site sounds narrower than the real business

The live site communicates a wider consulting and solutions business. The new site often reads as if AMJ only sells cybersecurity and Bitdefender.

### 3. The offer is inconsistent

The site alternates between:

- Demo Gratis
- Solicitar Demo
- Evaluacion Gratuita
- Contactanos

That weakens conversion because the visitor never sees one clear next step.

### 4. The proof is weak or disconnected

Some proof points appear decorative instead of tied to a buying decision.

### 5. Some sections read like template copy

Values, case studies, and generic solution cards do not yet sound like the real AMJ business.

## Rewrite Plan by Section

### Homepage Hero

Current job:
- Looks premium
- Says little

New job:
- State who AMJ helps
- State what AMJ does
- State the consultative offer
- Drive one CTA

Message structure:
- Headline: practical business value
- Subhead: consulting + implementation + support
- Proof strip: local support, years of experience, certified team
- CTA: free advisory or evaluation

### Philosophy Section

Current problem:
- Sounds aspirational, not operational

New job:
- Explain AMJ's working model: diagnose, recommend, implement, support

### Solutions / Services Overview

Current problem:
- Cards are broad and generic
- Service labels do not explain outcomes

New job:
- Present the offer as clear commercial tracks
- Tie each to a business pain and outcome

Recommended tracks:
- Consultoria en ciberseguridad
- Implementacion de soluciones de seguridad
- Software y plataformas empresariales

### Services Detail Section

Current problem:
- Reads like a list of capabilities without a buying frame

New job:
- Show what AMJ does, when to buy it, and what result the client gets

### Case Study / Credibility Section

Current problem:
- Risks sounding fabricated

New job:
- Use either real proof or a clearly-labeled implementation scenario
- Focus on challenge, intervention, and result

### Values / Why AMJ

Current problem:
- Generic brand adjectives

New job:
- Make each item a practical buying reason

Replace abstract values with:
- Equipo certificado
- Acompanamiento local
- Implementacion con continuidad operacional

### Product Hero

Current problem:
- Product-forward, but AMJ value is secondary

New job:
- Show the product clearly
- Explain that AMJ helps evaluate, deploy, configure, and operate it
- Keep "demo" only on this page if needed, but prefer "evaluacion" or "asesoria" elsewhere

### Product Capabilities

Current problem:
- Vendor-style feature copy

New job:
- Translate capabilities into business outcomes and operational use cases

### Plans

Current problem:
- Good structure, weak decision guidance

New job:
- Explain who each tier is for
- Reduce anxiety
- Reinforce AMJ support

### Contact

Current problem:
- Good form interaction, but copy should clarify what happens next

New job:
- Reduce friction
- State response expectation
- State what the prospect receives

## Success Criteria

The new copy should make a first-time visitor understand, within one screen:

- AMJ serves companies and institutions
- AMJ provides consulting plus implementation plus support
- AMJ specializes in cybersecurity and related enterprise technology solutions
- Contacting AMJ starts an advisory process, not a vague generic inquiry

## Execution Notes

- Rewrite the homepage to represent the full AMJ business.
- Rewrite the product page to represent one product inside that broader business.
- Use consistent CTA language.
- Remove or soften any copy that sounds fabricated or unverifiable.
